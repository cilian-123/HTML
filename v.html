V

Kim Tae-hyung (Korean: 김태형; born December 30, 1995),[1] also known professionally as V, is a South Korean singer and member of the boy band BTS.[2] Since his debut with the group in 2013, V has released three solo tracks under their name—"Stigma" in 2016, "Singularity" in 2018, and "Inner Child" in 2020—all of which charted on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart.
